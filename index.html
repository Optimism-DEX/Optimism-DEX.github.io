Why swap here

Optimism DEX for self-custody trading

Keep control from wallet to confirmation

Connect a self-custody wallet, choose the asset pair, and approve the swap only after the app presents the transaction details. Private keys stay with you, and the trade is signed from your wallet.

Built around L2 assets and routes

Trade OP, ETH, WETH, USDC stablecoin liquidity, and other supported assets surfaced in the app. Optimism keeps activity on an Ethereum layer two network while the app keeps the route readable.

Review cost, time, and route before signing

Every swap should be judged before it reaches your wallet prompt. Optimism DEX shows the live quote, route path, network cost, and expected timing in the app so you can confirm with context instead of guessing.

FAQ

Optimism DEX questions, answered

01

What is Optimism DEX?

Optimism DEX is a non-custodial DeFi app for swapping tokens on Optimism. It focuses on wallet-based trading, live route review, and clear confirmation before funds move.

02

Which chains and assets are supported?

The app is built for Optimism-focused swaps and supported OP Mainnet assets, including OP, ETH, WETH, USDC, and other tokens available in the app. See the full list on the supported chains page.

03

How long does a swap take?

Timing depends on wallet confirmation, network conditions, route complexity, and token approvals. The app shows expected timing before you sign so you can decide with current information.

04

How much does it cost to use?

Network costs and any route-specific costs are shown in the app before confirmation. You can also review completed transactions with an OP Mainnet explorer after signing.

05

Is Optimism DEX custodial?

No. You connect your wallet and sign transactions yourself. The app does not require a deposit account, and it does not take custody of your private keys.

06

What should I check before swapping?

Confirm the token contract, receiving asset, route, wallet network, slippage settings, and total cost. If anything looks wrong, reject the wallet prompt and restart the quote.
